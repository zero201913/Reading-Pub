Letter: So far, no intervention has been effective for obesity
信件：到目前为止，没有任何干预措施对肥胖有效

From Professor Arne Astrup, Senior Vice President, *Obesity and Nutritional Sciences*(肥胖与营养科学), *Novo Nordisk Foundation*(诺和诺德基金会), *Copenhagen*(哥本哈根), Denmark
来自阿恩·阿斯特鲁普教授，高级副总裁，肥胖与营养科学，诺和诺德基金会，丹麦哥本哈根

As part of our overall *ambition*(目标) of improving people's health, our main focus has been on *identifying*(识别) the *root causes*(根本原因) of *childhood obesity*(儿童肥胖), so as to be able to provide *robust evidence*(有力证据) to support effective *implementation*(实施). So far, no *intervention*(干预措施) has proved to be effective in real life to prevent *weight gain*(体重增加) and obesity among children, and it is obvious that there is a need for a better *understanding*(理解) of the complex *interaction*(相互作用) between *genetic markup*(基因标记), *early life exposures*(早期生活暴露) and *environmental factors*(环境因素).
作为我们改善人们健康的总体目标的一部分，我们的主要重点是识别儿童肥胖的根本原因，以便能够提供有力证据支持有效实施。到目前为止，还没有任何干预措施在现实生活中被证明能有效防止儿童体重增加和肥胖，显然我们需要更好地理解基因标记、早期生活暴露和环境因素之间的复杂相互作用。

We have *initiated*(启动) and *funded*(资助) several studies that test, for example, whether parents being *overweight*(超重) is *programming*(影响) their children to become obese; if too little sleep *predisposes*(倾向于) to weight gain; and a project that examines the effects of *healthy school meals*(健康学校餐食) with less *screen time*(屏幕时间) and more *physical activity*(体育活动) and optimal sleep. Moreover, together with the *Danish Ministry of Health*(丹麦卫生部), we have established *The Centre for Childhood Health*(儿童健康中心) - an independent centre supported by a *grant*(资助) of up to $1555 from the Novo Nordisk Foundation. The centre's main focus is to implement *preventive changes*(预防性改变) to ensure a *healthy weight*(健康体重) and the better *wellbeing*(健康) of all children in Denmark, and eventually to *identify solutions*(找出解决方案) with *global validity*(全球有效性).
我们已经启动并资助了几项研究，例如，测试父母超重是否会影响孩子变得肥胖；睡眠过少是否会导致体重增加；以及一个项目，研究健康学校餐食、减少屏幕时间、增加体育活动和优化睡眠的效果。此外，我们还与丹麦卫生部一起建立了儿童健康中心——一个由诺和诺德基金会提供高达1555美元资助的独立中心。该中心的主要重点是实施预防性改变，确保丹麦所有儿童的健康体重和更好的健康状况，并最终找出具有全球有效性的解决方案。

Worth noting is that although the Novo Nordisk Foundation owns about 28 percent of the shares in the *pharmaceutical company*(制药公司) *Novo Nordisk A/S*(诺和诺德公司), the two organisations are *individual entities*(独立实体) with separate *boards of directors*(董事会), *leadership teams*(领导团队) and goals. The foundation's *vision*(愿景) is to improve people's health and the *sustainability*(可持续性) of society and the planet.
值得注意的是，尽管诺和诺德基金会拥有制药公司诺和诺德A/S约28%的股份，但这两个组织是具有独立董事会、领导团队和目标的独立实体。该基金会的愿景是改善人们的健康以及社会和地球的可持续性。
