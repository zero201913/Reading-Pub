China's air-pollution control efforts drive investment worth 4 trln yuan
中国大气污染治理带动4万亿元投资

BEIJING, Dec. 11 (Xinhua) -- China's *air-pollution control efforts*(大气污染治理) have seen significant progress in recent years, driving the growth of the Chinese economy, according to the Ministry of Ecology and Environment on Monday.
北京，12月11日（新华社）——生态环境部周一表示，近年来中国的大气污染治理工作取得显著进展，推动了中国经济增长。

China's improvements in air quality are moving at the fastest rate globally, while the issue of *coal pollution*(煤炭污染) has been basically solved across the country, Liu Bingjiang, chief engineer with the ministry, told a press conference.
生态环境部总工程师刘炳江在新闻发布会上表示，中国空气质量改善速度全球最快，全国煤炭污染问题已基本解决。

China's efforts targeting air-pollution control have driven a total investment of about 4 trillion yuan (about 562.09 billion U.S. dollars) across the country, Liu said, adding that the efforts have also driven China's GDP to grow by about 5 trillion yuan.
刘炳江表示，中国大气污染治理工作已带动全国约4万亿元（约5620.9亿美元）的投资，同时推动中国GDP增长约5万亿元。

The process of improving air quality has seen the broad application of new technologies and products independently developed by China, which has promoted the development of a slew of relevant industries, including the manufacturing of *environmental protection equipment*(环保设备) and *new energy vehicles*(新能源汽车), Liu said.
刘炳江指出，在空气质量改善过程中，中国自主研发的新技术、新产品得到广泛应用，推动了包括环保设备制造和新能源汽车在内的一系列相关产业发展。

Meanwhile, a total of more than 3 million new urban jobs were created thanks to China's efforts on improving air quality, according to the ministry.
同时，据生态环境部统计，中国改善空气质量的努力已创造了超过300万个城镇新增就业岗位。
