Bird flu kills thousands of sea birds, marine animals in Peru
秘鲁发生禽流感，数千只海鸟和海洋动物死亡

Scientists do not rule out transmission of potentially deadly virus to humans
科学家不排除这种潜在致命病毒会传染给人类

An outbreak of *bird flu*(禽流感) is causing significant losses in several species of *wildlife*(野生动物) in Peru.
秘鲁爆发的禽流感已导致多种野生动物数量锐减。

The *H5N1 strain*(H5N1型禽流感病毒) has killed thousands of *sea lions*(海狮) and *pelicans*(鹈鹕) in *protected areas*(保护区) across the country, authorities said.
有关部门表示，H5N1型禽流感病毒已在全国多个保护区导致数千只海狮和鹈鹕死亡。

On Jan. 27, a hundred dead sea lions were found floating in the waters of Isla Asia, about 60 miles (96 kilometers) south of the capital Lima. Some 716 sea lions have been found dead on various beaches around the country.
1月27日，在首都利马以南约60英里（96公里）的亚洲岛海域发现了一百只死亡的海狮。全国各海滩已发现约716只死亡的海狮。

Since November, the virus has killed 63,000 birds, according to government data.
据政府数据显示，自去年11月以来，该病毒已导致6.3万只鸟类死亡。

Peru's agricultural health agency has reported that the disease may be transmitted by *migratory birds*(候鸟) from North America.
秘鲁农业健康机构报告称，这种疾病可能是由来自北美的候鸟传播的。

Authorities are worried as some of the species that are becoming infected are already considered *endangered*(濒危物种) in the country.
令有关部门担忧的是，部分感染该病毒的物种在秘鲁已被列为濒危物种。

In Peru, there are indications that the disease is spreading among *mammals*(哺乳动物) of different species.
在秘鲁，有迹象表明该疾病正在不同物种的哺乳动物之间传播。

Scientists fear that *close contact*(密切接触) between sea lions and humans increases the chances of the virus being *transmitted*(传播) to people.
科学家担心，海狮与人类的密切接触会增加病毒传播给人类的风险。

Peru's National Forest and Wildlife Service has urged people to avoid approaching sea lions and sea birds on the beach and to prevent their pets from doing so.
秘鲁国家森林和野生动物服务局已敦促人们避免在海滩上靠近海狮和海鸟，并防止宠物靠近它们。

In South America, bird flu cases have been detected in Ecuador, Bolivia, Chile, Paraguay and recently in Argentina and Uruguay, where a *sanitary emergency*(卫生紧急状态) has been declared.
在南美洲，厄瓜多尔、玻利维亚、智利、巴拉圭都已发现禽流感病例，最近阿根廷和乌拉圭也出现了病例，乌拉圭已宣布进入卫生紧急状态。

The director general of the *World Health Organization (WHO)*(世界卫生组织), Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesyus, said the virus has been spreading among *poultry*(家禽) and wild birds for 25 years, but recently infections have been detected in *minks*(水貂), *otters*(水獭) and sea lions, so "they should be closely monitored."
世界卫生组织（WHO）总干事谭德塞·阿达诺姆·格布雷耶苏斯表示，该病毒在家禽和野生鸟类中传播已有25年历史，但最近在水貂、水獭和海狮中也发现了感染病例，因此"应密切监测这些动物"。

Europe has experienced its *worst-ever outbreak*(有史以来最严重的疫情) of bird flu since 2021, while North and South America have recently been experiencing *severe outbreaks*(严重的疫情爆发).
自2021年以来，欧洲经历了有史以来最严重的禽流感疫情，而北美和南美最近也出现了严重的疫情爆发。
