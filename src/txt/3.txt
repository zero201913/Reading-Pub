Best apples to eat? Healthiest types from green to red and everything in between
吃哪种苹果最好？从青到红，各种颜色中最健康的品种

Apples are a *classic*(经典的) fruit choice. They're the *unofficial mascot*(非官方吉祥物) of lunchbox side dishes and they make a delicious afternoon snack when *dipped in*(蘸上) peanut butter. They're even available at McDonald's as a healthy Happy Meal side.
苹果是一种经典的水果选择。它们是午餐盒配菜的“非官方吉祥物”，蘸上花生酱后更是美味的下午茶点。你甚至可以在麦当劳买到它们，作为开心乐园餐的健康配菜。

It's no surprise then that the U.S is one of the world's largest apple *producer*(生产国), second only to China. We produce an average of 240 million *bushels*(蒲式耳) of apples every year, according to the U.S. Apple Association.
因此，美国是世界上最大的苹果生产国之一，仅次于中国，也就不足为奇了。根据美国苹果协会的数据，我们平均每年生产2.4亿蒲式耳的苹果。

But with so many *varieties*(品种) to choose from, is there one that *reigns supreme*(占据主导地位)? Here's what we found out about the *nutritional profiles*(营养成分) of the different kinds of apples.
但是，面对如此多可供选择的品种，是否有一种是“王者”呢？以下是我们对不同种类苹果营养成分的调查结果。

What is the healthiest apple?
哪种苹果最健康？

Whether it's *Granny Smith*(澳洲青苹), *Golden Delicious*(金帅), *Honeycrisp*(蜜脆) or *Gala*(嘎啦果); any type of apple is going to benefit your health. Only 12.3% of *American*(美国) adults meet their fruit intake *recommendations*(建议), the Center for Disease Control and Prevention found. The more apples in your diet, the better, says registered *dietitian*(营养师) Danielle Crumble Smith, regardless of the type. Some, however have unique health benefits.
无论是澳洲青苹（Granny Smith）、金帅（Golden Delicious）、蜜脆（Honeycrisp）还是嘎啦果（Gala），任何种类的苹果都对你的健康有益。美国疾病控制与预防中心发现，只有12.3%的美国成年人达到了建议的水果摄入量。注册营养师丹妮尔·克伦布尔·史密斯（Danielle Crumble Smith）表示，无论哪种类型，饮食中的苹果越多越好。不过，有些品种确实具有独特的健康益处。

*Red Delicious*(红蛇果) apples are the healthiest from an *antioxidant*(抗氧化剂) standpoint, Crumble Smith says. The darker the color of the apple, the richer it'll be in antioxidants. Antioxidants help *neutralize*(中和) the *free radicals*(自由基) in our bodies which, when imbalanced, can cause cell damage and lead to disease.
克伦布尔·史密斯说，从抗氧化剂的角度来看，红蛇果（Red Delicious）是最健康的。苹果的颜色越深，其抗氧化剂含量就越丰富。抗氧化剂有助于中和我们体内的自由基，当自由基失衡时，会导致细胞损伤并引发疾病。

Aiming for different types of deep colors can help you ensure you're getting *variety*(多种) of different types of antioxidants, she says.
她说，目标是不同种类的深色苹果，可以帮助你确保摄入多种不同类型的抗氧化剂。

If you're looking to increase your *vitamin C*(维生素C) intake, try a *Pink Lady*(粉红佳人) or *McIntosh*(麦金托什) apple, Crumble Smith says, If *fiber*(纤维) is what your seek, go for a Honeycrisp or Granny Smith.
克伦布尔·史密斯说，如果你想增加维生素C的摄入量，可以尝试粉红佳人（Pink Lady）或麦金托什（McIntosh）苹果；如果你寻求的是纤维，那就选择蜜脆（Honeycrisp）或澳洲青苹（Granny Smith）。

*Across the board*(全面地) with apples one thing's for sure, Crumble Smith, eat them *raw*(生吃) and *unpeeled*(不削皮).
关于苹果，有一件事是肯定的，克伦布尔·史密斯说：要生吃，而且不要削皮。

Eating the skin ensures that you're really going to eat all of that fiber, get all of those antioxidants because the skin is where a lot of it is, Crumble Smith says. The vitamin C content can also decrease during the *cooking process*(烹饪过程) because vitamin C is *soluble in water*(溶于水) and sensitive to temperature.
克伦布尔·史密斯说，吃果皮可以确保你摄入全部的纤维和所有的抗氧化剂，因为果皮中含有大量的这些成分。此外，在烹饪过程中，维生素C的含量也会下降，因为维生素C溶于水且对温度敏感。

Are apples healthy?
苹果健康吗？

Yes - apples are *packed with*(富含) vitamins and minerals and are an important part of a healthy diet, just like other fruits. They're a good source of fiber and contain *pectin*(果胶), a fiber that acts as a *prebiotic*(益生元) to aid *colon function*(结肠功能) and *digestion*(消化).
是的——苹果富含维生素和矿物质，就像其他水果一样，是健康饮食的重要组成部分。它们是良好的纤维来源，并含有果胶，这种纤维作为一种益生元，有助于结肠功能和消化。

Apples are high in vitamin C, which has antioxidant properties to help us absorb and store *iron*(铁), maintain *strong tissues*(强壮的组织), *boost our immune system*(增强免疫系统) and produce more *collagen*(胶原蛋白) for hair, skin and nail health.
苹果富含维生素C，维生素C具有抗氧化特性，有助于我们吸收和储存铁，维持强壮的组织，增强免疫系统，并产生更多胶原蛋白，促进头发、皮肤和指甲的健康。

Apples also contain *quercetin*(槲皮素), a *flavonoid*(类黄酮) with antioxidant properties that studies show may reduce the risk of *cardiovascular disease*(心血管疾病) and protect against allergies because it acts as a natural *antihistamine*(抗组胺药).
苹果还含有槲皮素（quercetin），这是一种具有抗氧化特性的类黄酮。研究表明，它可能降低心血管疾病的风险，并预防过敏，因为它起到了天然抗组胺药的作用。

*Apple cider vinegar*(苹果醋), a *fermented solution*(发酵溶液) made from pressed apples, also has numerous health benefits including weight loss and *cholesterol management*(胆固醇管理).
苹果醋是一种由压榨苹果制成的发酵溶液，也具有许多健康益处，包括减肥和胆固醇管理。

Apples are listed on the *Dirty Dozen list*(“脏十二种”名单), or the *Environmental Working Group's*(环境工作组) analysis of the 12 vegetables with the highest traces of *pesticides*(农药).
苹果被列入了“脏十二种”名单（Dirty Dozen list），即环境工作组（Environmental Working Group）分析出的农药残留量最高的12种蔬菜（和水果）之一。

This doesn't means you shouldn't eat them. Crumble Smith says. Buying *organic*(有机的) is one option if you're eating apples daily. Still, organic produce is not completely *pesticide-free*(无农药) because farmers often use naturally-derived pesticides derived. Research published in the Journal of Toxicology found the pesticides in the produce on the Dirty Dozen list are at *negligible levels*(可忽略不计的水平) and substituting organic didn't result in significant risk reduction.
克伦布尔·史密斯说，这并不意味着你不应该吃它们。如果你每天都吃苹果，购买有机苹果是一个选择。不过，有机农产品并非完全没有农药，因为农民经常使用天然衍生的农药。发表在《毒理学杂志》（Journal of Toxicology）上的研究发现，“脏十二种”名单上农产品中的农药含量可以忽略不计，用有机产品替代并不会显著降低风险。
